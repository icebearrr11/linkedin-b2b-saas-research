# Video Transcript Summary: Chris Walker on Demand Generation

## Topic
- **Core Concept**: Why B2B companies are failing at lead gen and why they should pivot to demand generation.

## Key Takeaways
- **Dark Social**: Most buying decisions happen in "dark social" (communities, DMs, private conversations) that CRMs can't track.
- **Content Strategy**: Don't gate your content. Provide value upfront to build authority.
- **The Shift**: Marketing shouldn't just be about capturing leads; it should be about creating demand before a prospect ever visits your website.
