# Video Transcript Summary: Wes Bush on Product-Led Growth (PLG)

## Topic
- **Core Concept**: How to use your product as your primary growth engine.

## Key Takeaways
- **Value First**: Ensure the user experiences "Time to Value" (TTV) as quickly as possible.
- **Freemium vs. Trial**: Choose a model that matches how customers actually want to buy your product.
- **Alignment**: Product, marketing, and sales must all be aligned on the product-led vision.
