# Video Transcript Summary: Dave Gerhardt on B2B Brand Building

## Topic
- **Core Concept**: Transitioning from "Lead Gen" tactics to "Brand Building" for long-term growth.

## Key Takeaways
- **Brand vs. Performance**: Performance marketing is for quick wins; brand marketing is the moat that protects your company.
- **Storytelling**: People buy from people. Use your brand voice to build trust before you ask for a sale.
- **Consistency**: Brand is not a logo; it is the sum of every interaction a customer has with your company.
