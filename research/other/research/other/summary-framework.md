## 2. The LinkedIn Daily Checklist
A synthesized routine based on expert workflows:

- [ ] **Content Creation (Morning)**: Draft one high-signal post using a "hook-body-CTA" framework (Welsh/Gerhardt).
- [ ] **Direct Engagement (15-30 mins)**: Reply to comments on your own posts to build community (Clark).
- [ ] **Proactive Outreach (15-30 mins)**: Engage with content from target audience/industry leaders (Walker).
- [ ] **Brand Check**: Ensure your profile clearly communicates the value proposition and "why" you are different (Laja/Richardson).

## 3. The Content Engine Framework
1. **Define the Problem**: Start with a specific pain point (Wes Bush).
2. **Challenge the Status Quo**: Explain why traditional ways of solving this fail (Chris Walker).
3. **Provide Actionable Value**: Share a framework, template, or personal lesson (Emily Kramer).
4. **Be Human**: Use empathy and personal stories to connect (Sara Lattanzio).
5. **Optimize for Distribution**: Keep sentences short, punchy, and scannable (Giles Richardson).
