# Analysis: Wes Bush - Product-Led Growth (PLG)

**Author**: Wes Bush
**Topic**: Product-Led Growth Strategy
**Source**: LinkedIn

## Key Takeaways
- **Product as Salesman**: In a PLG model, the product itself is the main driver of acquisition, retention, and expansion.
- **Time to Value (TTV)**: The primary goal is to help users experience the "Aha! moment" as quickly as possible. Every friction point in the onboarding process is a potential lost customer.
- **The "Freemium" Trap**: Don't offer a free plan just because it's popular. Ensure your product has clear boundaries between the free and paid versions that make upgrading a logical next step.

## My Analysis
Wes Bush’s philosophy is transformative for modern SaaS. By focusing on the product's ability to sell itself, companies can drastically reduce their CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) and create a more scalable growth engine compared to traditional, sales-heavy models.
