# Analysis: Giles Richardson - SaaS Copywriting

**Author**: Giles Richardson
**Topic**: High-Conversion SaaS Copywriting
**Source**: LinkedIn

## Key Takeaways
- **Clarity over Cleverness**: In SaaS, don't try to be "witty" or "poetic." Your copy should be crystal clear. The reader should understand what you do and why it helps them within seconds.
- **Pain-Point Focused**: Great copywriting starts by articulating the reader’s pain better than they can describe it themselves. When they feel "heard," they are more likely to trust your solution.
- **The "So What?" Test**: Every sentence in your copy must answer the reader's subconscious question: "So what? How does this benefit me?"

## My Analysis
Giles Richardson’s focus on clarity is a breath of fresh air. In a crowded B2B SaaS market, being the easiest to understand is often a bigger competitive advantage than having the most features.
