# B2B SaaS LinkedIn Content Strategy Research

This project is a research-based repository dedicated to analyzing organic LinkedIn content strategies for B2B SaaS growth.

## Project Goal
To collect, organize, and analyze high-signal content from 10 proven industry practitioners to build an actionable playbook for LinkedIn organic growth.

## Repository Structure
- `/research/sources.md`: Curated list of 10 industry experts.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/`: Collection of high-performing LinkedIn posts analyzed for tactics.
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/`: Transcripts from expert-led videos for deep-dive learning.
- `/research/other/`: Additional research materials and notes.

## Methodology
1. **Selection**: Identifying experts who are active practitioners in the B2B SaaS space.
2. **Collection**: Gathering recent high-signal content (posts and transcripts).
3. **Analysis**: Extracting actionable frameworks and patterns from the collected data.

## Why These Experts?
These experts were selected because they are **active practitioners**—not just theorists. They are currently building in public and testing these strategies on their own LinkedIn profiles and for their B2B SaaS clients, providing battle-tested insights.
